Frequently Asked Questions

What is the government subsidy for solar panels?

The government provides 70-80% subsidy on solar panel installation for residential homes and up to 100% for certain categories like schools and hospitals. The subsidy amount varies based on state policies and the capacity of the system being installed.

How long does solar panel installation take?

Typically, installation takes 1-2 weeks from the day we begin. This includes site assessment, design, material procurement, installation, and grid connection. The timeline may vary depending on the project size and government approvals.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide 25-year performance warranty on solar panels, 5-year warranty on inverters, and 10-year warranty on electrical equipment. Our maintenance and monitoring services ensure optimal performance throughout the warranty period.

What roof types are eligible for solar installation?

Ideal roofs are south-facing with minimal shading from trees or adjacent buildings. We can install on concrete, tile, metal, and other roof types. Our experts will conduct a free site assessment to determine suitability.

What's the actual cost after government subsidy?

After 70-80% government subsidy, costs typically start from ₹30,000 per kW for residential installations. For commercial and institutional buildings, the effective cost can be even lower with 100% subsidy availability in many cases.

What's the installation process?

Our process: Site Visit → Engineering Design → Quote Approval → Subsidy Application → Government Approval → Material Purchase → Installation → Testing → Grid Connection → Commissioning. We handle all paperwork and government approvals.

How much maintenance is required?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. We recommend one annual inspection and cleaning. Our monitoring system alerts you to any issues. We offer annual maintenance packages for complete peace of mind.

What's the return on investment (ROI) period?

Most customers achieve payback within 3-5 years through electricity bill savings. After that, you enjoy 20+ years of free or nearly-free electricity, resulting in significant long-term savings of ₹5-10 lakhs.
